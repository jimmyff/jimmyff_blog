Museum sketching

Met up with some friends today and spent the afternoon chatting away whilst doing some sketching at the Leeds City Museum. This was a statue of Dorothy Una Ratcliffe (A Yorkshire poet). Homemade Christmas Cards!

One November Saturday morning I did this Santa drawing with the idea to make our first ever homemade Christmas Cards! Neil came up with a poem for the inside of the cards. The hardest bit was I stuck 10 little gem stickers on each of the baubles to give them a bit of sparkle. I ended up sticking over 1000 gems on with tweasers! 😅 We chose to support two very deserving charities: Mermaids & UK black pride....
